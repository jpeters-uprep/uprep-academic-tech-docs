# VR Software

These VR Apps are available using our Quest 3 headsets.
Reach out to June (jpeters@universityprep.org) for classroom or Community Time access.

## Wander

Travel anywhere in the world in full immersive street view! See the Taj Mahal, the Dawn Wall at Yosemite, or the local streets of an upcoming Global Link trip.

## Human Anatomy VR

Explore anatomy in detail with this excellent app. Great for biology, PE, or detailed understanding of our bodies.

## YouTube

YouTube on Quest includes the ability to watch standard videos in large screen format or immersive videos that are designed for VR.

## Cubism

Challenge your mind in Cubism, a deceptively simple puzzle game where you assemble increasingly complex shapes out of colorful blocks.

## Walkabout Mini Golf

"Putt around" on 14 unique 18-hole courses.
