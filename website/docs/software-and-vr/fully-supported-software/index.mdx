---
title: Fully Supported Software
---

Supported software includes a premium license, in some cases paid and in other
cases free for education. IT and Academic Technology are here to support the
UPrep Community in using this software.

## Adobe Creative Cloud

We offer a wide range of Adobe products to meet many art, design, and
engineering needs. See the [Creative Cloud page](./adobe-creative-cloud) for
more information.

## AutoDesk CAD and Design

We have licenses for AutoDesk software, including Fusion, Tinkercad, Revit, and
more. See the [AutoDesk page](./autodesk) for more information.

## Bambu Studio

Our fleet of Bambu 3D printers are powered by Bambu Studio, a desktop app on our
Makerspace PCs that takes 3D models from Fusion, Tinkercad, or online model
sources like [printables.com](https://www.printables.com/) and gets them ready
to send to the printers.

## Blooket

Website: [Blooket](https://www.blooket.com)

Blooket is a game-based learning platform where students compete in review games
to earn points and in-game rewards. Used in World Language classes.

## Canva

[Canva](https://www.canva.com/) is a design platform, allowing you to create
banners, signs, slideshows, and more. It even lets you use AI to create several
starter designs based on your detailed requirements. It is free for all faculty
and students.

## Conjuguemos

Website: [Conjuguemos](https://conjuguemos.com)

Conjuguemos is an online practice platform for foreign language grammar,
vocabulary, and verb conjugation drills. Used in World Language classes.

## Delta Math

Website: [Delta Math](https://www.deltamath.com)

Delta Math is an online math practice and assessment platform with auto-graded
problem sets aligned to course content. Used across the Math department.

## Gimkit

Website: [Gimkit](https://www.gimkit.com)

Gimkit is a game-based quiz platform where students earn in-game currency for
correct answers, similar to Kahoot. Used in Science and World Language classes.

## Google Workspace

[Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/my-drive), Docs, Sheets, and
Slides are great for collaboratively creating classic documents.

## Graph Free

Website: [Graph Free](https://www.graphfree.com)

Graph Free is an online graphing tool used for creating and annotating graphs in
math instruction.

## HMH Digital

Website: [HMH](https://www.hmhco.com)

HMH Digital provides Houghton Mifflin Harcourt's digital textbooks and
curriculum materials, funded through the textbook budget for select Math
courses.

## iChinese

Website: [iChinese](https://www.ichineselearning.com)

iChinese is a digital Chinese language learning curriculum used in World
Language classes.

## MakeMusic

Website: [MakeMusic](https://www.makemusic.com)

MakeMusic (SmartMusic) is music practice and assessment software that lets band
and orchestra students record and submit practice assignments for feedback.

## Math Bits

Website: [MathBitsNotebook](https://mathbitsnotebook.com)

MathBitsNotebook provides online practice resources and activities for Algebra
and Geometry courses.

## NoRedInk

Website: [NoRedInk](https://www.noredink.com)

NoRedInk is a web-based platform for grammar, writing, and vocabulary practice
with personalized, adaptive exercises. Used across the English department.

## No Zombies Warmup

Website: [No Zombies Warmup](https://www.nozombiesworkout.com)

No Zombies Warmup is a digital library of warm-up and workout routines used by
PE teachers and coaches.

## Padlet

Website: [Padlet](https://padlet.com)

Padlet is a visual collaboration board for brainstorming, discussions, and
resource sharing. UPrep's paid subscription is limited to a handful of approved
users; everyone else can still use Padlet's free tier.

## Poll Everywhere

Website: [Poll Everywhere](https://www.polleverywhere.com)

Poll Everywhere is a real-time audience response tool — students respond to
polls from a phone or laptop for live classroom engagement.

## Quizlet

Website: [Quizlet](https://quizlet.com)

Quizlet is an online study platform with flashcards, practice tests, and games
for memorization and review. Used in Science and World Language classes.

## Runestone Academy

Website: [Runestone Academy](https://runestone.academy)

Runestone Academy provides interactive online textbooks and a practice
environment for Computer Science courses.

## Schoology

From courses to clubs, we run a lot of our school on
[Schoology](https://universityprep.schoology.com/home).

## Screencastify

Website: [Screencastify](https://www.screencastify.com)

Screencastify is a screen recording tool used to create video tutorials and
flipped-classroom content.

## Sibelius

Website: [Sibelius](https://www.avid.com/sibelius)

Sibelius is professional music notation software used for composing and
arranging sheet music.

## Silhouette Studio

Our Silhouette vinyl cutter is powered by Silhouette Studio on our ULab
Makerspace PC. It lets you take a vector image (SVG) and send it to the cutter.

## Spotify

Website: [Spotify](https://www.spotify.com)

Spotify Premium is used for music streaming and training playlists, primarily in
PE and Athletics.

## Veracross

We manage schedules, class registration, and official grades on
[Veracross](https://portals.veracross.com/uprep).

## Volt Athletics

Website: [Volt Athletics](https://www.voltathletics.com)

Volt Athletics is a strength and conditioning platform that provides custom
training plans for student athletes.
