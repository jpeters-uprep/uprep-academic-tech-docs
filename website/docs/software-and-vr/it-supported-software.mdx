---
title: IT Supported Software
---

These core tools are managed and supported directly by IT, rather than Academic
Technology.

## AirTame

AirTame lets you wirelessly cast your screen to classroom displays, no cables or
dongles required.

## Zoom

Zoom Pro licenses are available for all faculty and staff. Students have basic
(free) Zoom accounts.

## Microsoft Office (M365)

The full Microsoft 365 suite — Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams — is
available to all faculty, staff, and students.

## Google Workspace

[Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/my-drive), Docs, Sheets, and
Slides are available to the whole UPrep community for collaboration and file
storage.

Visit the [IT Support page](https://support.universityprep.org/) for more
information.
