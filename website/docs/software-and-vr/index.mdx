## Academic Software at UPrep

We have many software tools available at UPrep for students, faculty, and staff.
Some software is [officially licensed by UPrep](./fully-supported-software),
including Schoology, Veracross, Adobe Creative Cloud, Microsoft 365 and Copilot,
Canva, Autodesk, and more. Then there is some
[recommended free software](./recommended-free-software) that we provide light
support for, including [......]. Any other software is
[not supported](unsupported-software).

Faculty and Staff may request support for additional software by following the
process on the Academic Tech for Faculty site (linked from Veracross).

Core infrastructure tools like AirTame, Zoom, Microsoft Office (M365), and
Google Workspace are managed directly by IT rather than Academic Technology —
see [IT-Supported Software](./it-supported-software) for details.

## Quest VR Headsets

In addition to software, we have two sets of 9 Quest 3 headsets. Teachers may
request VR use in their classrooms by reaching out to June Peters at
jpeters@universityprep.org. Our list of [VR software](./vr-software) is
available for reference as well.
