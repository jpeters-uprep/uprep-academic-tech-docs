# Welcome

This site is designed to help students, faculty, and staff understand the
digital and physical tools they have access to for classes, teams, clubs, and
community time. It provides information on our Makers tools, Apps, and VR.

To join the 3D printing queue or upload files to print yourself, go
[here](https://sites.google.com/universityprep.org/makers/home).

## Makers Not Spaces

Our philosophy of Academic Technology centers on people. We are focused on
students and what they want to make and learn. "Makerspaces" tend to be what
people talk about, but we're much more interested in Makers.

For help with Academic Technology, reach out to June Peters -
jpeters@universityprep.org
