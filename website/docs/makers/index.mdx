Utilizing the Upper School and Middle School Makerspace, the Library, and
Stagecraft, UPrep offers many [tools](./makers-tools) and
[experiences](./programs-teams-and-clubs) for Makers. Students, faculty, and
staff can learn, explore, and build across a wide range of mediums from 3D
printing to weaving.

## Makers Not Spaces

Our philosophy centers on people. We are focused on students and what they want
to make and learn. "Makerspaces" tend to be what people talk about (and we've
got great spaces), but we're much more interested in Makers.
