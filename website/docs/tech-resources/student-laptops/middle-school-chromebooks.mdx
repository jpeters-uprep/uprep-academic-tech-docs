Middle School students are required to have a Chromebook for school work.

Visit the [Chromebook Setup Guide](../chromebook-setup) for related information.

## Recommended Models

As a parent, you want to make sure that your child has the tools they need for
learning. There are many different Chromebook models available, so you can
choose one that best meets your child's needs. Google has created a new, simple
standard called Chrome Plus for quality Chromebooks that should last for 3+
years, supporting all of Middle School. We recommend the devices that meet the
**Chromebook Plus** requirements and have a **touchscreen**.

### Some factors to consider when choosing a Chromebook for your child include:

- Price: Chromebook Plus devices range in price from around $370 to $800
- Screen: 14" is more portable than larger screens and recommended for most
  students. Ensure your device has a touchscreen.
- Battery life: Chromebooks last 10+ hours on a single charge.
- Weight: most 14" Chromebook Plus models weigh less than 3.5 pounds.
- Warranty: we recommend a 3 year warranty with accidental damage protection to
  ensure the device lasts for all of Middle School.

### ALTERNATIVES

If you decide to buy a smaller or older device, please ensure the following
requirements are met:

- 768p+ HD Touchscreen
- 8GB RAM
- 10+ hour battery life
- 128GB+ hard drive
- 720p+ Webcam

## Where to Buy

There are several options for Chromebook Plus devices from manufacturers
including HP, Lenovo, and Asus. You can see the current list on the
[Chromebook website](https://www.google.com/chromebook/shop-chromebooks/?chromebookTier=Chromebook+plus),
and you’re welcome to buy them there, from the manufacturer, Amazon (ensure they
are sold by an authorized retailer), Best Buy, or another authorized retailer.
