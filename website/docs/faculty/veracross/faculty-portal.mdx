There are separate portals on Veracross for Faculty/Staff, Students, Parents,
and the Board. The Faculty Portal is at
https://portals.veracross.com/uprep/faculty. If you belong to more than one of
these groups, you may switch between the portals using the Veracross icon at the
top:

<img
  src={useBaseUrl("/img/veracross/facultyPortal.png")}
  alt="Faculty Portal selector"
/>

On the faculty portal, you will see your schedule, classes, and links. To the
right, you'll see a sidebar with the directory and other links.
