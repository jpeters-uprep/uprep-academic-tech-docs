---
title: Expired/Doesn't Exist
---

## Problem

You get an error like "this course has expired" or "this content doesn't exist"

## Fix

1. Visit the [Schoology](https://universityprep.schoology.com/home) homepage
2. Log in
3. Try your original link again

## Notes

Schoology doesn't manage login properly so if your authentication has expired,
the error is confusing.
