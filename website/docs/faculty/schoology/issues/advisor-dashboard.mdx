## Question

As an advisor, how do I check in on my advisees?

## Answer

Click Tools in the top menu, then Advisor Dashboard. Search for an advisee by
name, and you'll see the same summary view they (and, in Middle School, their
families) see: current course enrollments, grades, attendance, upcoming
assignments, and calendar. It's a fast way to check whether an advisee is
falling behind before a check-in meeting.

See Schoology's
[Advisors and Advisees documentation](https://uc.powerschool-docs.com/en/schoology/latest/advisors-and-advisees-on-schoology)
for full details.
