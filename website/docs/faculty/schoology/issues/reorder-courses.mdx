---
title: My Course List Is Too Long
---

## Question

I have a lot of courses and can't find the one I need in the Courses menu.

## Answer

The Courses drop-down menu only shows your first 12 courses. Click My Courses →
Re-order Courses to drag your most important courses to the top, or use the
Course Dashboard on your Schoology home page, where you can also drag and drop
course tiles into any order you like.

For one-time search, use the search section on the right in My Courses to narrow
down by teacher, course name, semester, etc.
