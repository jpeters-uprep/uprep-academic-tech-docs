---
title: A Student Added or Dropped My Class
---

## Question

A student's schedule changed after the start of the year — do I need to do
anything in Schoology?

## Answer

Yes. Veracross is always the official roster; Schoology does not
automatically sync mid-year schedule changes. If a student adds or drops your
course, go to the Members tab of your Schoology course and add or remove them
yourself so your Schoology roster matches Veracross.

Two things to know: students can't remove themselves from a course, and a
student can't be added to a new section of a class while still enrolled in an
old section of the same class — if you can't find a student in the add box,
check whether they're still enrolled in another section first.
