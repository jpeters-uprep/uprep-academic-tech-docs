title: Software Process
---

We have many [software tools](../../../software-and-vr) available at UPrep for
students, faculty, and staff. Some software is officially licensed by UPrep,
including Schoology, Veracross, Adobe Creative Cloud, Microsoft 365 and Copilot,
Canva, Autodesk, and more.

We have an annual process for requesting licenses for software that is not
currently supported:

In mid-semester two, department chairs will notify their departments that we
have started the process of reviewing software requests for the upcoming school
year.

A deadline for requests will be set in ILT and faculty will be notified of the
deadline.

Department chairs will gather requests and put information on spreadsheet.

All requests will be reviewed in ILT. Faculty will be notified of decisions by
department chairs.

ILT will share spreadsheet with Amy (for budgeting purposes) and June (FYI
purposes).

We are in the process of moving away from reviewing requests outside of this
process. Exceptional circumstances should be requested through department
chairs, who should talk to division heads.
