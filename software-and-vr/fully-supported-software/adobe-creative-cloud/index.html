Adobe Creative Cloud

Upper School UPrep students and faculty have access to Adobe Creative Cloud, a professional suite of design, media, and creative tools. Middle School students have access if they are in certain classes such as photo, yearbook, or journalism. Log in with your school email at https://creativecloud.adobe.com.

Design & Illustration​

Photoshop — Edit and enhance photos, create digital art, and composite images.

Illustrator — Design vector graphics, logos, and illustrations that scale beautifully.

InDesign — Create posters, magazines, and multi-page documents for print or digital use.

Express (formerly Spark) — Make quick graphics, flyers, and videos in your browser.

Fresco — Digital drawing and painting app for tablets and stylus use.

Photography​

Lightroom — Edit and organize photos with easy, professional controls.

Lightroom Classic — Advanced photo cataloging and editing for desktop workflows.

Bridge — Browse, organize, and batch-rename Creative Cloud assets.

Video, Audio & Motion​

Premiere Pro — Full-featured video editing software for film and social media.

Premiere Rush — Simplified video editor for quick projects.

After Effects — Add motion graphics, titles, and visual effects.

Audition — Record, mix, and edit audio and voiceovers.

Media Encoder — Export and compress video and audio for sharing.

Web, Animation & UX​

Animate — Create 2D animations and interactive media for web or video.

Dreamweaver — Build and edit responsive websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Adobe XD — Design and prototype user interfaces and apps.

3D & Immersive​

Substance 3D Painter / Stager / Sampler / Designer — Tools for creating and rendering 3D textures, materials, and scenes.

Aero — Design and preview augmented reality (AR) experiences.

PDF, AI & Utility Tools​

Acrobat Pro — Create, edit, and combine PDFs; add fillable forms and signatures.

Firefly (Generative AI) — Create AI-assisted images, text effects, and design assets safely in the browser.

Character Animator — Bring illustrated characters to life with motion capture.

Capture (mobile) — Turn photos into color palettes, brushes, and vector shapes.

Cloud & Collaboration​

Creative Cloud Libraries — Save and share assets across apps.

Adobe Fonts — Thousands of fonts for Creative Cloud projects.

Adobe Stock — Royalty-free photos, graphics, and video clips for school use.

Get Started​

Visit https://creativecloud.adobe.com

Sign in with your @uprep.org email and choose Company or School Account.

Install the apps you need, or use Express, Firefly, and Photoshop Web directly in your browser.
