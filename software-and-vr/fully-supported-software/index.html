Fully Supported Software

Supported software includes a premium license, in some cases paid and in other cases free for education. IT and Academic Technology are here to support the UPrep Community in using this software.

Adobe Creative Cloud​

We offer a wide range of Adobe products to meet many art, design, and engineering needs. See the Creative Cloud page for more information.

AutoDesk CAD and Design​

We have licenses for AutoDesk software, including Fusion, Tinkercad, Revit, and more. See the AutoDesk page for more information.

Bambu Studio​

Our fleet of Bambu 3D printers are powered by Bambu Studio, a desktop app on our Makerspace PCs that takes 3D models from Fusion, Tinkercad, or online model sources like printables.com and gets them ready to send to the printers.

Canva​

Canva is a design platform, allowing you to create banners, signs, slideshows, and more. It even lets you use AI to create several starter designs based on your detailed requirements. It is free for all faculty and students.

Google Workspace​

Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Slides are great for collaboratively creating classic documents.

IXL​

XL is a comprehensive, standards-aligned educational platform that provides adaptive practice and real-time analytics for K-12 subjects, including math, language arts, science, and social studies. It empowers teachers to deliver personalized instruction through skill-based recommendations while helping students master concepts at their own pace with immediate feedback. https://www.ixl.com/

Schoology​

From courses to clubs, we run a lot of our school on Schoology.

Silhouette Studio​

Our Silhouette vinyl cutter is powered by Silhouette Studio on our ULab Makerspace PC. It lets you take a vector image (SVG) and send it to the cutter.

Veracross​

We manage schedules, class registration, and official grades on Veracross.
