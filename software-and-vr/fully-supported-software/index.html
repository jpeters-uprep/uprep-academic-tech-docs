Fully Supported Software

Supported software includes a premium license, in some cases paid and in other cases free for education. IT and Academic Technology are here to support the UPrep Community in using this software.

Adobe Creative Cloud​

We offer a wide range of Adobe products to meet many art, design, and engineering needs. See the Creative Cloud page for more information.

AutoDesk CAD and Design​

We have licenses for AutoDesk software, including Fusion, Tinkercad, Revit, and more. See the AutoDesk page for more information.

Bambu Studio​

Our fleet of Bambu 3D printers is powered by Bambu Studio, a desktop app on our Makerspace PCs that takes 3D models from Fusion, Tinkercad, or online model sources like printables.com and gets them ready to send to the printers.

Blooket​

Website: Blooket

Blooket is a game-based learning platform where students compete in review games to earn points and in-game rewards. Used in World Language classes.

Canva​

Canva is a design platform, allowing you to create banners, signs, slideshows, and more. It even lets you use AI to create several starter designs based on your detailed requirements. It is free for all faculty and students.

Conjuguemos​

Website: Conjuguemos

Conjuguemos is an online practice platform for foreign language grammar, vocabulary, and verb conjugation drills. Used in World Language classes.

Delta Math​

Website: Delta Math

Delta Math is an online math practice and assessment platform with auto-graded problem sets aligned to course content. Used across the Math department.

Gimkit​

Website: Gimkit

Gimkit is a game-based quiz platform where students earn in-game currency for correct answers, similar to Kahoot. Used in Science and World Language classes.

Google Workspace​

Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Slides are great for collaboratively creating classic documents.

Graph Free​

Website: Graph Free

Graph Free is an online graphing tool used for creating and annotating graphs in math instruction.

HMH Digital​

Website: HMH

HMH Digital provides Houghton Mifflin Harcourt's digital textbooks and curriculum materials, funded through the textbook budget for select Math courses.

iChinese​

Website: iChinese

iChinese is a digital Chinese language learning curriculum used in World Language classes.

MakeMusic​

Website: MakeMusic

MakeMusic (SmartMusic) is music practice and assessment software that lets band and orchestra students record and submit practice assignments for feedback.

Math Bits​

Website: MathBitsNotebook

MathBitsNotebook provides online practice resources and activities for Algebra and Geometry courses.

NoRedInk​

Website: NoRedInk

NoRedInk is a web-based platform for grammar, writing, and vocabulary practice with personalized, adaptive exercises. Used across the English department.

No Zombies Warmup​

Website: No Zombies Warmup

No Zombies Warmup is a digital library of warm-up and workout routines used by PE teachers and coaches.

Padlet​

Website: Padlet

Padlet is a visual collaboration board for brainstorming, discussions, and resource sharing. UPrep's paid subscription is limited to a handful of approved users; everyone else can still use Padlet's free tier.

Poll Everywhere​

Website: Poll Everywhere

Poll Everywhere is a real-time audience response tool — students respond to polls from a phone or laptop for live classroom engagement.

Quizlet​

Website: Quizlet

Quizlet is an online study platform with flashcards, practice tests, and games for memorization and review. Used in Science and World Language classes.

Runestone Academy​

Website: Runestone Academy

Runestone Academy provides interactive online textbooks and a practice environment for Computer Science courses.

Schoology​

From courses to clubs, we run a lot of our school on Schoology.

Screencastify​

Website: Screencastify

Screencastify is a screen recording tool used to create video tutorials and flipped-classroom content.

Sibelius​

Website: Sibelius

Sibelius is professional music notation software used for composing and arranging sheet music.

Silhouette Studio​

Our Silhouette vinyl cutter is powered by Silhouette Studio on our ULab Makerspace PC. It lets you take a vector image (SVG) and send it to the cutter.

Spotify​

Website: Spotify

Spotify Premium is used for music streaming and training playlists, primarily in PE and Athletics.

Veracross​

We manage schedules, class registration, and official grades on Veracross.

Volt Athletics​

Website: Volt Athletics

Volt Athletics is a strength and conditioning platform that provides custom training plans for student athletes.
